About us

The circumstances for undocumented students in higher education have drastically improved over the past decade, but there is still work to do. We believe that all undocumented students deserve to have the same support in their academic endeavors. We believe that access to a higher education should be accessible regardless of citizenship status or economic background. Here at “My education, My journey” we help transform lives by providing you with the resources and support you need.

Yes! YOU can go to college!

We designed a quiz to assist folks in realizing their alternatives. Depending on your immigration status, there are a sizable number of scholarships available to assist you. Give as honest of an answer as you can to a few questions, then the algorithm will give you scholarships tailored to you.
